üéØ Problem Statement: Learning ASL can be challenging due to the lack of real-time feedback and limited opportunities for conversational practice, especially for learners outside of hard-of-hearing communities or classroom settings.

Motivation

Despite over 500,000 people in the U.S. using ASL to communicate, most popular language-learning platforms do not support ASL practice. Our goal with SignBridge was to create an interactive, personalized learning platform that leverages AI/ML to help learners practice ASL accurately and receive real-time feedback.

Key Features

Personalized Quiz

After signing up, users take a personalized quiz. This lets us understand their goals and prior ASL knowledge so we can suggest the right resources and lessons.

Dashboard

A central hub where users can check their ASL learning progress, jump into lessons, and view their progress in relation to others.

Progress Page

Users can see their progress more in-depth on the progress page. To gamify the ASL learning experience, we offer checkpoints after users complete a certain amount of lessons.

Lessons

Users can view their upcoming lessons and get started.
